## Context Content

Projects have a fixed timeline, clear deadlines, and specific tasks you need to complete.
Use the project-home template to have a guide.

Most people have five to fifteen projects going on at the same time. Projects can be anything from writing a book to developing an app or learning a new language.
When it comes to work related project, I recommend to not exceed 2-3 projects per quarter.
